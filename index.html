“Local Knowledge” is the greatest resource an adventurer can find. The residents of a destination know the best spots for beautiful vistas and rarely seen artifacts. They can relate history and legend with the passion of native culture. Finding this resource has always been challenging, and the proliferation of tour group packages has stifled the local flavor of exploring. Now you can go straight to the source, find local guides whose experience and expertise is a perfect match for the type of experience you are seeking. Wanderlust is the connection between you and the cultures of the world, sign up today and get to know the real world.

A modern map to discovery

Wanderlust is an online based research tool for finding local guides at destinations all around the world. No matter where you are accessing the site from, or on what device, all of your information travels with you. Your arrangements, itineraries, and plans are all safely stored and easily accessible. Wanderlust is also a platform for local guides to offer their services and be easily found through our powerful search tools. This puts the people who live in amazing destinations back in charge of sharing their homes and cultures with the world, and helps keep the economic benefits local.
